    ZBB ERP Module Implementation¶

    Workshop Date: Month 1, Week 2, Day 3

    Executive Summary¶

All functional requirements sections were reviewed, validated, and prioritized. 15 gaps identified and 8 requirement changes documented. Updated FRD to be circulated within 1 week.

    • ✅ All 7 FRD sections reviewed and validated
    • ✅ 15 gaps identified with proposed solutions
    • ✅ Requirements prioritized using MoSCoW method
    • ✅ Stakeholder consensus achieved

    Session Summaries¶

    Session 1: Introduction and Context ✅¶

    Duration: 30 minutes

    Key Discussion Points: - Current Excel-based process pain points confirmed - Budget cycle time: 8-10 weeks (target: 4-6 weeks) - Consolidation effort: 40+ hours per cycle - Version control issues causing errors

    Outcome: Strong alignment on business case and project objectives

    Session 2: System Integration & Architecture ✅¶

    Duration: 60 minutes

    Validated: - ✅ Chart of Accounts structure (847 accounts confirmed) - ✅ Multi-dimensional requirements (Cost Center, Business Unit, Product, Geography) - ✅ GL integration approach (REST API - PoC successful) - ✅ 6 user roles (Executive, Budget Team, Unit Heads, Operations, Finance, Auditors)

    Gaps Identified:

    GAP-001: Product Dimension Missing - Description: Product dimension not included in CoA mapping - Impact: High - Cannot track budget by product line - Proposed Solution: Add Product dimension to CoA mapping - Priority: Must Have - Owner: Functional Lead

    GAP-002: Delegation Functionality - Description: No provision for temporary delegation of approval authority - Impact: Medium - Workflow bottlenecks during leave - Proposed Solution: Add delegation feature to RBAC - Priority: Should Have - Owner: Technical Lead

    Session 3: Assumptions Engine & ZBB Logic ✅¶

    Duration: 75 minutes

    Validated: - ✅ Assumption categories (Macroeconomic, Growth, Regulatory, Operational) - ✅ Version control approach - ✅ Scenario support (Base, Optimistic, Pessimistic) - ✅ Mandatory justification (50 character minimum)

    Changes Requested:

    CHANGE-001: Justification Character Limit - Current: 50 characters minimum - Requested: 100 characters minimum - Rationale: 50 characters insufficient for meaningful justification - Decision: ✅ Approved - Update to 100 characters - Impact: Low - UI change only

    CHANGE-002: Document Attachment Threshold - Current: $50,000 - Requested: $25,000 - Rationale: Lower threshold for better governance - Decision: ✅ Approved - Update to $25,000 - Impact: Low - Configuration change

    GAP-003: Assumption Approval Workflow - Description: Unclear who approves assumption changes - Impact: High - Governance gap - Proposed Solution: CFO approves all assumption changes - Priority: Must Have - Owner: Functional Lead

    Session 4: Loan Portfolio & Funding Modules ✅¶

    Duration: 90 minutes

    Validated: - ✅ Product types taxonomy (Retail, SME, Corporate, Mortgage, Consumer) - ✅ PFI classification - ✅ Monthly phasing approach - ✅ Interest income calculations - ✅ IFRS 9 provisioning logic

    Gaps Identified:

    GAP-004: Loan Repricing Assumptions - Description: No provision for loan repricing assumptions (interest rate changes) - Impact: High - Affects interest income projections - Proposed Solution: Add repricing schedule to loan module - Priority: Must Have - Owner: Functional Lead

    GAP-005: Early Repayment Assumptions - Description: No provision for early repayment rates - Impact: Medium - Affects loan portfolio projections - Proposed Solution: Add early repayment rate parameter - Priority: Should Have - Owner: Business Analyst

    CHANGE-003: Provisioning Stages - Current: 3 stages (Stage 1, 2, 3) - Requested: Add Stage 3 sub-categories (Specific, General) - Rationale: Regulatory reporting requirement - Decision: ✅ Approved - Impact: Medium - Data model change

    Session 5: Financial Statements & KPIs ✅¶

    Duration: 75 minutes

    Validated: - ✅ Income Statement structure (15 line items) - ✅ Balance Sheet structure (22 line items) - ✅ Cash Flow Statement (indirect method) - ✅ All 13 KPI formulas mathematically correct

    KPI Target Ranges Defined:

    KPI Target Range Alert Threshold
    Net Interest Margin (NIM) 3.5% - 4.5% <3.0%
    Return on Assets (ROA) 1.2% - 1.8% <1.0%
    Return on Equity (ROE) 12% - 18% <10%
    Cost-to-Income Ratio 45% - 55% >60%
    Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR) 75% - 85% >90% or <70%
    NPL Ratio <3% >5%
    Provision Coverage Ratio >100% <80%
    Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR) >12% <10%

    Gaps Identified:

    GAP-006: Additional KPIs - Description: Request for 3 additional KPIs (Liquidity Coverage Ratio, Net Stable Funding Ratio, Leverage Ratio) - Impact: Medium - Regulatory reporting - Proposed Solution: Add 3 KPIs to engine - Priority: Should Have - Owner: Functional Lead

    CHANGE-004: Cash Flow Statement Method - Current: Indirect method - Requested: Also provide direct method option - Rationale: Some stakeholders prefer direct method - Decision: ⚠️ Deferred to Phase 2 (post-implementation) - Impact: High - Significant development effort

    Session 6: Workflow, Validation & Reporting ✅¶

    Duration: 60 minutes

    Validated: - ✅ 8-stage workflow appropriate - ✅ Approval hierarchy defined - ✅ Hard validations (9 rules) appropriate - ✅ Soft validations (6 warnings) appropriate

    Changes Requested:

    CHANGE-005: SLA Timers - Current: 3 days for review stage - Requested: 5 business days - Rationale: 3 days too tight for thorough review - Decision: ✅ Approved - Update to 5 business days - Impact: Low - Configuration change

    CHANGE-006: Budget Growth Warning Threshold - Current: 20% year-over-year growth triggers warning - Requested: 15% threshold - Rationale: Tighter control on budget growth - Decision: ✅ Approved - Update to 15% - Impact: Low - Configuration change

    GAP-007: Bulk Approval Functionality - Description: No provision for bulk approval of multiple budget submissions - Impact: Medium - Efficiency for approvers - Proposed Solution: Add bulk approval feature - Priority: Should Have - Owner: Technical Lead

    GAP-008: Mobile Approval - Description: Request for mobile approval capability - Impact: Low - Convenience feature - Proposed Solution: Defer to Phase 2 (out of scope for initial implementation) - Priority: Won't Have (Phase 1) - Owner: N/A

    Session 7: Wrap-up and Prioritization ✅¶

    Duration: 30 minutes

    MoSCoW Prioritization Results:

    Must Have (8 items): - GAP-001: Product dimension - GAP-003: Assumption approval workflow - GAP-004: Loan repricing assumptions - CHANGE-001: Justification 100 characters - CHANGE-002: Document attachment $25K - CHANGE-003: Provisioning sub-categories - CHANGE-005: SLA 5 business days - CHANGE-006: Budget growth 15% threshold

    Should Have (4 items): - GAP-002: Delegation functionality - GAP-005: Early repayment assumptions - GAP-006: Additional 3 KPIs - GAP-007: Bulk approval

    Could Have (0 items): - None identified

    Won't Have - Phase 1 (3 items): - GAP-008: Mobile approval - CHANGE-004: Direct method cash flow - GAP-009: Multi-currency support (identified but deferred)
