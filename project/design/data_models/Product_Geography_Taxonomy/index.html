    Budget Allocation Rules by Product¶

    Revenue Accounts¶

    • Must have Product dimension: All revenue accounts require product assignment
    • Examples:
    • Interest Income from Loans â†’ PROD-R04 (Personal Loans) or PROD-S01 (SME Loans)
    • Fee Income from Accounts â†’ PROD-R01 (Savings) or PROD-R02 (Current)

    Direct Expense Accounts¶

    • Must have Product dimension: Direct expenses tied to specific products
    • Examples:
    • Loan Loss Provisions â†’ Product-specific
    • Product Marketing Expenses â†’ Product-specific
    • Product Development Costs â†’ Product-specific

    Overhead Expense Accounts¶

    • Product dimension optional: Shared overhead not allocated to products
    • Examples:
    • Head Office Salaries â†’ No product assignment
    • IT Infrastructure â†’ No product assignment (or allocated to PROD-D01/D02)
