    Loan Product Taxonomy¶

    ZBB ERP Module - Loan Portfolio Module¶

    Document Type: Technical Specification
    Version: 1.0
    Status: Approved
    Date: Month 4, Week 1

    Overview¶

    This document defines the loan product taxonomy for the ZBB ERP Module, covering 15 loan products across 5 categories with detailed attributes for budgeting and IFRS 9 provisioning.

    Product Categories (5)¶

    1. Retail Banking¶

    Target Customers: Individual consumers
    Characteristics: Small ticket size, high volume, standardized terms

    2. SME (Small & Medium Enterprises)¶

    Target Customers: Small and medium businesses
    Characteristics: Medium ticket size, moderate volume, semi-customized terms

    3. Corporate Banking¶

    Target Customers: Large corporations
    Characteristics: Large ticket size, low volume, highly customized terms

    4. Mortgage Loans¶

    Target Customers: Individual homebuyers
    Characteristics: Long tenor, secured by property, standardized terms

    5. Consumer Finance¶

    Target Customers: Individual consumers
    Characteristics: Small ticket size, short tenor, higher interest rates

    IFRS 9 PFI Classification¶

    Stage 1: Performing (12-month ECL)¶

    Criteria: Current on payments, no significant increase in credit risk
    Days Past Due: 0-30 days
    Provision Rate: 0.5% - 2.0% (varies by product)

    Stage 2: Underperforming (Lifetime ECL)¶

    Criteria: Significant increase in credit risk but not credit-impaired
    Days Past Due: 31-90 days
    Provision Rate: 5.0% - 15.0% (varies by product)

    Stage 3: Non-Performing (Lifetime ECL, credit-impaired)¶

    Criteria: Credit-impaired, default likely
    Days Past Due: >90 days
    Provision Rate: 25.0% - 100.0% (varies by product and collateral)

    Product Activation Status¶

    All 15 products are ACTIVE and available for budgeting.

    Integration with GL¶

    Each loan product maps to a specific GL account for interest income recognition: - 1010-1012: Retail Banking - 1020-1022: SME - 1030-1032: Corporate Banking - 1040-1042: Mortgage Loans - 1050-1052: Consumer Finance

    Provision expense is recorded in: - 5100: Provision Expense - Loan Losses (all products)
